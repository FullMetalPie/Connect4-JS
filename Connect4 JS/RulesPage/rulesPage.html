Rules

How to play the game

1. Game setup:
The game is played on a 6x7 grid (6 rows and 7 columns).
Each player chooses a color (usually red or yellow) and takes turns placing one of their colored discs into any of the empty columns.

2. Taking Turns:
Players take turns placing one of their discs into any open column from the top.
Once a disc is dropped into a column, it occupies the lowest available space in that column.

3. Connecting Four
The objective is to be the first player to connect four of their own discs in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).
The connection can be made in any direction (left, right, up, down, or diagonally).
